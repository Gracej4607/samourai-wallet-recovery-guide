## 🔑 Recovering your funds

The recovery process involves restoring your wallet files using your specific secret words.

### Sparrow Wallet setup
1. Open Sparrow Wallet.
2. Select File and then New Wallet.
3. Name your wallet.
4. Select Seed Words in the Keystore section.
5. Choose 12 or 24 words based on your seed phrase.
6. Enter your words carefully. Do not copy and paste these words. Type them manually to prevent errors.
7. Click the Passphrase button if you have one. Enter it exactly. Passphrases are case-sensitive.
8. Set a password for your wallet file. This prevents unauthorized access to your computer wallet file if someone gains access to your machine.
9. Wait for the wallet to calculate your address.

### Electrum setup
1. Open Electrum.
2. Name your wallet file.
3. Select Standard Wallet.
4. Choose I already have a seed.
5. Enter your words.
6. Click Options and select BIP39 seed if you created your wallet using standard Samourai settings.
7. Enter your passphrase in the next box if required.
8. Set a password for the wallet file.
9. Electrum will now scan the blockchain for your funds.

## 🔄 Dealing with Whirlpool UTXOs

Samourai Wallet used a technology called Whirlpool to mix bitcoin. These mixed coins behave differently than standard bitcoin. When you restore your wallet, you might not see the full balance immediately.

The software must scan the history of your wallet. This scan can take time. Keep your computer connected to the internet. Do not close the software while the progress bar shows movement.

If your balance remains zero, check your derivation path. Samourai Wallet often uses specific paths for its mixed coins. You may need to change the setting in your new wallet to match the path Samourai used. Consult the advanced settings section in the software if the funds do not appear within an hour.

## 📄 Frequently asked questions

### Will my funds be lost forever?
No. Your funds live on the blockchain, not in the app. As long as you have your seed phrase, you can access your bitcoin.

### What if I forgot my passphrase?
The passphrase acts as a secret key. If you lose it, you cannot recover the wallet. There is no password reset for a seed phrase.

### Can I move funds to a hardware wallet?
Yes. Once you see your balance in Sparrow or Electrum, you can send your bitcoin to a hardware wallet address. This increases the security of your funds.

### What does the DOJ case mean for me?
The legal situation involves the creators of Samourai Wallet. It does not change the way the Bitcoin network works. The software you use to recover your coins is separate from the company that built Samourai.
